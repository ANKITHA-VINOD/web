Youth for Sustainable Development

May 1 | 2020 

The world today looks forward to saviors and warriors who could be the torch bearers for a new planet where all the needs of it's residents are fulfilled,at the same time taking care that none of their greed are completed and also making sure that we leave enough for the generations to come. Someone who has a lot of energy,new ideas, courage and wisdom. So whom are we talking about? Yes,the youth.They are undoubtedly the last ray of hope for a better world as they have the power to bring about better economic and social reforms as the they acquired wisdom from the examples of great leaders who lived before them.

The present situation of the world is pathetic by all means - poverty, hunger, diseases, inequality, pollution, economic crisis, greed, climatic changes and what not! So the task that lies ahead of the present generation isn't easy, it's tough,but not impossible.Young leaders must be carved into responsible humans concerned and willing to help people not expecting gigantic material benefits in return.The young energy must be channelized to address and understand the present dark realities of humans so that they could come out with elaborate,effective and long term solutions, standing against inequalities.

Creating responsible World Leaders

We need to mould our youth into responsible world leaders by giving them exposure , showing them the dim scenarios of the world today and teaching them to live by their needs and not greed and stand strong against the black winds of corruption through deep analysis and strong opinions in political,social and economic issues and spread the message of caring for our mother earth, parallely meeting the needs of the coming generations

They must find reforms to end poverty and make sure there's no hunger,hence no more suffering and encourage agriculture and zero food wastage, making households self sufficient.They must stop traditions of lavish spendings and wastage during big fat weddings and ceremonies.They must be strong advocates against pollution due to our illogical and unplanned utilisation of commodities which is now leading to a lot of problems -the most dangerous being climate change. The drastic effects of climate change is slowly waking up as seen from the decrease of ozone layer and increase in sea level,the death of arctic organisms and unprecedented weather changes leading to life threatening situations like flood and land slides.One of the catalyst for climate change can unequivocally be attributed to human greed.So the prime battle that the youth must fight inorder to achieve a sustainable World is GREED.

To live by their needs and not greed and stand strong against the black winds of corruption through deep analysis and strong opinions in political,social and economic issues 

The non renewable sources are being exploited at an exponential rate and we all fear whether the coming generation will have to face the dire consequences.The only way to save the future lives is by taking actions right now by the right person. The brightness of the future is directly proportional to the empowerment of youth.

Let them lay the strong foundation for a sustainable World were people suffer less and smile more.
